Ava doesn't believe in the supernatural, but she loves a good story! Here are some of her favorite myths, urban legends, folklore creatures, and other tales of cryptids.

  • The Mothman
  • Sasquatch
  • The Goatman
  • The Jersey Devil
  • Squonk
  • Chupacabra
  • Skinwalkers

10/10. Mothman folklore is nice, but it's the culture surrounding him that makes him great. A festival, a 90s movie, a whole TikTok trend. Plus a statue of him that definitely proves he's into Crossfit.

Again, 10/10. Sasquatch has got variety; is he the spirit protector of the forest, like Native Americans claim? Or is he simply an undiscovered yet un-supernatural species? Another aspect of Sasquatch lore is how prevalent it is world-wide. The Pacific Northwest has Bigfoot, Florida has the Skunk-Ape, Nepal has the Yeti, Japan has the Hibagon, Indonesia has the Ebu Gogo. Really makes you wonder...

5/10. The Goatman of Devil's Bridge is nothing but fodder for legend-tripping teenagers - but then, is that a bad thing? Added points for him being featured on a Buzzfeed Unsolved episode though.

8/10. The only bad thing about the Jersey Devil is the poor guy's stuck in New Jersey. Other than that though, I have a hunch that he and Mothman are one in the same. On what grounds? Just a gut feeling.

9/10 for the poor little Squonk. We all love a guy who's not afraid to show his sensitive side... My only problem is that I never heard about him sooner.

7/10. "Animal kills livestock" isn't exactly the most riveting origin story for a cryptid, but I'm here for some latino representation in this list.

10/10. My Grandma on the rez says that the police get calls about "The Enemies" all the time. Scary stuff right there.
